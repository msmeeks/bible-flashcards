ESV Audio Playback

Hearing the real Crossway recording during the text phase of audio review for ESV verses, with a silent fallback to text-to-speech when offline.

Start Review Play

From the Review tab, a session of memorized verses is queued in Play mode, including at least one ESV verse.

Review → Play → Start
2

Reference, then pause

The reference is spoken by the device's text-to-speech voice, followed by a timed pause for mental recitation.

"Romans 8:28" · (pause for recitation)
3

Real ESV recording plays

Because this verse is saved as ESV, the text phase plays the actual Crossway audio recording fetched and cached by the app, rather than a synthesized voice.

▶
Now Playing — Romans 8:28 (ESV recording)
4

Silent fallback when offline

If the recording can't be fetched (no connection, cache miss, server error), playback falls back to the synthesized voice automatically — no error message interrupts the session.

Offline → same text phase, spoken by text-to-speech instead
